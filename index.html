  1. In Settings, scroll to Security & privacy and tap it.

  5. Select PIN.
  6. Set and confirm your new PIN.

Tip: Use at least a six‑digit PIN—ideally eight to ten digits; a long alphanumeric password provides stronger protection.

  1. Swipe down from the top of the screen.

  5. In the UNKNOWN profile, change its PIN as in Step 3.
  6. IMPORTANT: Use a different PIN for each user profile.

Tip: You can exit the secondary profile at any time by pressing and holding the power button and tapping 'End session'.
